who am i?

A beauty based photographer, professional career and founder of Brice Ali photography company .

I'm a photographer based in Kisumu and Nairobi, Kenya who specializes in photographing beauty; I also have a mild car obsession. I grew up in Kisumu, Kenya: a prolific large land-laked city that's one part laked based and one part dramatic city fairy tale. Here I was lucky enough to meet a number of amazing professionals who opened my eyes to the world of art and design extending my stay to Nairobi; this would alter the path of my career in more ways than I could ever imagine. I have a studio in both Kisumu and Nairobi.

After studying studio Art and Design at the University of Buruburu Institute of Fine Arts,Nairobi I moved back to Kisumu, in hopes of becoming a professional photographer and skateboard profession .

A couple of years and too many injuries later, I found myself taking up an offer to photograph a few events and families for various clients I'd met while recuperating. What started by chance turned out to be the perfect mix of technical challenge and creative outlet. In 2018, I founded the Brice Ali Photography , a resource for beauty photographers and those in the photography industry seeking to place memories about the photos and theory of beauty photography(studio based shoots).
